The Circle of Ray: A Few Good Women

This is a visualization of the male-female ratio of credited actors in the films of Satyajit Ray (1921-1992). All actors listed on IMDb are included.

Indian Cinema's Babel

This notebook is a visualization of Indian films. The first 'sound' films were released in 1931. Between 2018 and 2019, India produced 2,446 films in 55 languages and dialects.

Moretti: British Novel Genres

This notebook visualizes a dataset from Franco Moretti's Graphs, Maps, Trees: Abstract Models for Literary History (2007). The graphs feature many different British novel genres.

25 Heroes of Hindi Cinema, 1931-2020

This project is more playful than a comprehensive study: a visualization of the number of films featuring 25 top male Bollywood stars of the Hindi film industry.

West Bengal Assembly Elections

The datasets connected with page and Election Commission Reports constitute resources aimed at a long view of electoral politics in West Bengal.

Where the UIUC Religion Alumni Are

The Department of Religion at the University of Illinois will celebrate its golden jubilee in 2021. The department's alumni are spread over 32 states.
